Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress and creates value by transforming ideas into solutions. Embracing technology and creativity unlocks new opportunities for growth and development.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures that natural resources are preserved and utilized responsibly. By focusing on environmental stewardship, we can secure a healthier future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure is the backbone of a thriving economy. Investing in robust transportation networks, energy systems, and digital connectivity lays the foundation for prosperity.

Empowerment

Empowerment empowers individuals and communities to take control of their destinies. By fostering education, equality, and inclusivity, we can build a more just and dynamic society.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

We need to mobilize our intellectual, financial, and youth power to lay the foundation for a new civilization. Our mission is to restore democracy, combat corruption, and overhaul key sectors such as the judiciary, banking, and education. We must work together to build a just and inclusive society, free from corruption and oppression. The sacrifices made during the recent uprising must translate into a just and democratic society.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
